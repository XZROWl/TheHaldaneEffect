About Us

Welcome to the official website of The Haldane Effect! We are a Southampton-based indie band, although we have our influences,

we feel like we've built our own sound, we all love it and we hope you do too.

If you want to hear more, then you're in luck! As our debut album Kookbox is out now!!

We're here to be all yours, your lawfully wedded band, The Haldane Effect.
