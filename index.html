The Haldane Effect


Crawling from the underworld of the UK’s South Coast, The Haldane Effect encompass a tasteful yet malicious blend of post rock storytelling and neo-psychedelic soundscapes. Featuring piercing noise passages that play with and pulverise your soul, pieced back together by progressive rock movements. The band are always ambitious to grow into their best musical forms, and to subvert the expectations of modern music, their upcoming sophomore album is to be an exceptional representation of these concepts.
