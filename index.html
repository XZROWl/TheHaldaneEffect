About Us

Welcome to the official website of The Haldane Effect! We are a Southampton-based indie band influenced by bands like Arctic Monkeys, The Smiths, and The Stone Roses.

Tired of everything we hear on the radio? Yearning for the return of real music, albums, physical releases, proper followings of proper bands?

We feel like we've built our own sound, we all love it and we hope you do too.

If you want to hear more, then it's just a short wait as we've got our debut album in the making right now!

This is what we are here to bring, we have more in stock with character, humor, and real people as your soundtrack. We're here to be all yours, your lawfully wedded band, The Haldane Effect.
