About Us

Welcome to the official website of The Haldane Effect, a Southampton-based indie band with a sound that's uniquely ours.

We draw from countless influences, yet what we create feels like home—dreamy, honest, loud and tender.

Our debut album Kookbox is out now. It’s yours if you want it. Let’s make noise together.
