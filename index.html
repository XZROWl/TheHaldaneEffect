About Us

Welcome to the official website of The Haldane Effect, a Southampton-based indie band with a sound that's unique like no other.

We draw from countless influences, yet what we create something of our own.

Our debut album Kookbox is out now. It’s yours if you want it. Let’s make noise together.

We're 5 who are 1. And also 1 who are 5. Thank you and goodnight.
