About Us

Welcome to the official website of The Haldane Effect! We are a Small emerging southamptopn based indie band influenced by bands like Arctic Monkeys, The Smiths and The Stone Roses.

Tired of everything we hear on the radio? Yearning for the return of real music, albums, physical releases, proper followings of proper bands?

This is what we are here to bring, we have more in stock with character, humour and real people as your soundtrack. We're here to be all yours, your lawfully wedded band,

The Haldane Effect.
